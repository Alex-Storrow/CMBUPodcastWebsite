Chat My Bitch Up is a female-ran, Liverpool-based podcast. We devised this project with the aim of celebrating and championing independent music and culture within our city. Liverpool is a melting pot of creativty and a certified music hub, there is so much to see and engage with. This is your brand-new, one-stop shop to discover what's going on in the city from underground music, culture, art, fashion and theatre.
